# Health Charted

An AI research partner for your health. It learns your situation, helps you understand it, and builds a knowledge base that gets richer over time.

Everything lives on your computer. Plain text files you own. No account, no app, no company.

**Website:** healthcharted.com

## What you can do with this

**Understand what's going on with your body.** What your lab results mean for your specific situation, how they compare to previous results, and what to watch for next.

**Research any health topic from the ground up.** The system traces mechanisms, cites sources, and shows you the actual evidence. When something comes up, you get the biology, not a summary.

**Walk into appointments prepared.** Specific questions based on your data. After the visit, everything gets captured and connected to what the system already knows.

**Keep track of everything in one place.** Labs, medications, symptoms, provider visits, decisions, and the reasoning behind them. The system connects things across months and years.

**Manage health for your family.** Each person gets their own folder.

## Getting started

You need [Claude Desktop](https://claude.ai/download) with a Pro plan ($20/month).

1. Download Health Charted (green **Code** button above, then **Download ZIP**)
2. Unzip it somewhere on your computer
3. Open Claude Desktop, click the **Code** tab, and select the folder you just unzipped
4. Start a conversation. You might say "I'd like to set up my health profile" or just describe what's going on with your health. The system takes it from there.

Health Charted is built for Claude Desktop. It works with other AI platforms that can read and write local files, but setting that up requires some technical comfort.

## How it works

You talk to it about your health. It asks questions, keeps track of everything, and connects it over time. A lab result today gets checked against your results from six months ago. A new medication gets checked against everything else you're taking.

It traces the biology, not just the recommendation. When evidence is strong, it says so. When researchers disagree, it tells you that too. When its analysis differs from what your doctor said, it shows you both perspectives. You decide what to do.

When something changes, the system identifies which parts of your health picture might need revisiting and walks you through it. When there's a gap in what it knows, it tells you.

The longer you use it, the more useful it gets.

## Research tools

For deeper research with cited sources, the system can connect to the Perplexity API, which searches the web, academic literature, news, and other sources. Perplexity is a paid service with per-use pricing. The system walks you through connecting it when you're ready. Details and pricing at [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai).

Health Charted has no affiliation with any tools or services mentioned here. These are what have worked best so far. As better tools become available, the system will integrate them.

## Built to improve

Health Charted is open source and community-supported. When improvements are available, the system offers to update itself. Updates enhance the system's capabilities without affecting your health data.
